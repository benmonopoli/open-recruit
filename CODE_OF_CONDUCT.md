# Code of Conduct

## The short version

Be respectful and constructive. This is a practitioner tool built to be useful — contributions and discussions should stay focused on making it better.

## Expected behaviour

- Be direct and specific in feedback — vague criticism isn't helpful
- Respect that others have different hiring contexts, markets, and experience
- Accept that not every contribution will be merged — quality and fit matter
- Give credit where it's due if you build on someone else's work

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Personal attacks, insults, or harassment of any kind
- Dismissing contributions based on who made them rather than their quality
- Sharing private information about anyone without their permission
- Any conduct that a reasonable person would consider unprofessional

## Enforcement

This project is maintained by one person. If something isn't right, contact me directly at **ben.monopoli@ahrefs.com**. I'll review it and respond.

Contributions or comments that don't meet these standards may be removed or rejected without extended discussion.
