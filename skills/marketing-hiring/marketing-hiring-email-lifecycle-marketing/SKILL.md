## Universal Seniority Framework

| Level | Typical YoE | What they own | Scope |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Coordinator / Associate** | 0–2 years | Executes defined tasks, supports campaigns | Self |
| **Manager / Specialist** | 2–5 years | Owns a channel or programme end-to-end | Channel / function |
| **Senior Manager / Senior** | 5–8 years | Drives strategy for a channel, leads small teams or agencies | Team / function |
| **Director** | 8–12 years | Owns a marketing function (e.g. demand gen, brand), manages managers | Department |
| **VP / Head of Marketing** | 12+ years | Full marketing function ownership, budget, exec reporting | Company |
| **CMO** | 15+ years | Company-wide marketing strategy, board-level, often P&L exposure | Company + external |

**Marketing-specific promotion signal:** Moving from execution to strategy. The jump from Senior to Director is often the hardest — it requires comfort with ambiguity, managing through influence, and operating with incomplete data.

## Email & Lifecycle Marketing

### What This Role Does
Email and lifecycle marketers drive revenue and retention through systematic, personalised communication across the customer journey: onboarding, activation, engagement, upsell, and winback. The role spans campaign strategy, automation design, copywriting, segmentation, and deliverability. At SaaS companies, lifecycle marketing directly impacts activation rates and net revenue retention — one of the highest-ROI marketing disciplines.

### Titles & Synonyms

| Level | Common titles |
|---|---|
| Specialist | Email Marketing Specialist, CRM Specialist, Lifecycle Marketing Coordinator |
| Manager | Email Marketing Manager, Lifecycle Marketing Manager, CRM Manager |
| Senior | Senior Lifecycle Marketing Manager, Senior CRM Manager, Retention Marketing Manager |
| Director | Head of CRM, Director of Lifecycle Marketing, Head of Retention |

### Skill Tiers

**Tier 1 — Must-have:**
- ESP/CRM platforms: HubSpot, Klaviyo, Braze, Iterable, Customer.io, or Mailchimp — hands-on campaign and automation experience
- Segmentation: behavioural, firmographic, lifecycle-stage segmentation — not just list blasts
- Copywriting: email subject lines, preview text, body copy, CTAs — understands what drives opens and clicks
- A/B testing: subject lines, send times, content, calls to action
- Deliverability basics: SPF/DKIM/DMARC, spam triggers, list hygiene, unsubscribe management

**Tier 2 — Differentiator:**
- Automation design: multi-step nurture flows, trigger-based sequences, lifecycle journey mapping
- Personalisation: dynamic content, merge tags, behaviour-triggered sends
- Email analytics: open rate (with MPP context), click rate, conversion, revenue per email, list churn
- Onboarding sequence design: activation email flows for SaaS products
- GDPR/CAN-SPAM compliance: consent management, preference centres

**Tier 3 — Bonus:**
- SQL or API integration for custom segmentation
- Predictive send-time optimisation
- SMS/push notification integration alongside email
- Email design (HTML/CSS templates) — not required but valuable
- Revenue attribution from email at scale

### Red Flags
- Measures email success by open rate only — especially post-Apple Mail Privacy Protection
- No segmentation experience — one-size-fits-all blasts
- No automation/flow design — campaign-only, no lifecycle thinking
- Cannot speak to deliverability — has caused domain blacklisting or doesn't know what it means
- No GDPR awareness for roles serving European audiences
