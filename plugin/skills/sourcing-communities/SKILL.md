## Primary Sourcing Communities

### SourceCon
[sourcecon.com](https://www.sourcecon.com/)
The primary dedicated sourcing conference and community. Annual event (SourceCon 2025: San Diego, October).
- **Content:** Deep-dive sourcing sessions, live Boolean demos, AI tool walkthroughs
- **Notable practitioners:** Dean Da Costa, Steve Levy, Glen Cathey
- **SourceCon Academy:** Structured curriculum on search strings, LinkedIn hacks, social platform sourcing, contact finding — [eretraining.com/p/sourcecon-academy-course](https://www.eretraining.com/p/sourcecon-academy-course)
- **Community:** Active Slack and online community between events

### Boolean Strings Network
[booleanstrings.com](https://booleanstrings.com) | [booleanstrings.ning.com](https://booleanstrings.ning.com)
Founded by Irina Shamaeva — the canonical sourcing blog with 2M+ views. Irina stepped back from active posting in December 2024; David Galley now runs training.
- **300 Best Boolean Strings eBook (6th Ed.):** 300+ sample strings with detailed explanations — regularly updated
- **Ning community:** Global practitioners sharing new strings, discoveries, edge cases
- **LinkedIn Boolean Strings Network group:** Active discussion on new sourcing techniques

### WizardSourcer (Jonathan Kidder)
[wizardsourcer.com](https://wizardsourcer.com)
Active blog covering new tools, sourcing hacks, automation workflows.
- **[Free tools directory](https://wizardsourcer.com/tools/)** — maintained and updated regularly, one of the best free resources in sourcing
- **[Hacker News sourcing hack](https://wizardsourcer.com/sourcing-hack-for-hacker-news-site/)** — specific technique for sourcing from HN "Who is hiring?" and "Who wants to be hired?" threads
- **Top Talent Sourcing Tools Vol. 2** — covers AI/automation tools A-to-Z
- Perplexity AI for recruiters coverage (one of first blogs to cover it in depth)

### RecruitingDaily
[recruitingdaily.com](https://recruitingdaily.com)
Active tool reviews, sourcing content, and practitioner shows.
- **Dean Da Costa & Steve Levy monthly show** — "Dean and Steve's Most Excellent Sourcing Journey" — 70+ combined years of sourcing experience, covers AI tools, X-Ray hacks, new platform tricks
- **#HRTX events** — hands-on practitioner workshops
- Covers new tool launches and integrations regularly

### Sourcing Summit (SOSU)
International sourcing-focused conferences with Irina Shamaeva and other top practitioners. Content available post-event at [sosu.com](https://sosu.com).

## Reddit Communities for Sourcers

Reddit has become a meaningful sourcing and community channel. Key subreddits:

| Subreddit | Use |
|---|---|
| **r/recruiting** | Peer discussions, tool recommendations, TA professionals |
| **r/forhire** | Candidates explicitly listing availability — direct sourcing |
| **r/cscareerquestions** | Tech candidates discussing careers — expertise signals |
| **r/MachineLearning** | ML practitioners — identify expertise before reaching out |
| **r/datascience** | Data professionals community |
| **r/devops** | DevOps/infrastructure engineers |

**Reddit sourcing tactics:**
- Use `flair_name:"for hire" [city]` to find location-specific available candidates in r/forhire
- Build karma before posting jobs — communities ban low-karma accounts that immediately post roles
- When posting jobs, always include salary range — dramatically increases response quality
- Use `site:reddit.com/r/[subreddit] "I work at" OR "at my company"` to find practitioners to approach

## Hacker News Sourcing

HN runs two recurring threads worth bookmarking:
- **"Who is hiring?"** (monthly, first weekday) — companies posting roles directly
- **"Who wants to be hired?"** (monthly, first weekday) — candidates explicitly available

**Sourcing hack (via WizardSourcer):**
```
site:news.ycombinator.com "who wants to be hired" "machine learning" "remote"
site:news.ycombinator.com "who wants to be hired" "backend" "San Francisco" 2025
```

These threads are updated monthly and candidates posting here are typically high-quality engineers who are actively looking but not using job boards. Low competition sourcing channel.
